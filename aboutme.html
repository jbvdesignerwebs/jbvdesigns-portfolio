About Me

My name is Jasmine Boquer-Velasco (JBV), and I specialize in illustration and graphic design. I create visual work that is expressive, engaging, and grounded in strong visual identity and clear storytelling.

Originally from Oʻahu, Hawaiʻi, I earned my Associate’s degree in Digital Media Production from Leeward Community College (LCC) in Spring 2025 and am currently pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Graphic Design. During my time at LCC, I worked with Ka Manaʻo, the college’s student publication, as both a graphic designer and illustrator. I also created promotional materials for LCC’s Student Life, designing flyers, brochures, and other graphics that supported campus organizations, events, and student engagement.

Outside of design, I enjoy listening to music, taking walks, and spending time with cats; all of which often spark new ideas and creative inspiration.
