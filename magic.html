Magic Trick

Tool: Procreate

Summary

I was tasked to create a promotional comic for Ka Manaʻo's Spring 2025 magazine issue for social media. As this was my first opportunity to design promotional material for the publication, I aimed to create something simple, eye-catching, and aligned with the magazine’s established visual identity.

After reviewing the Spring 2025 issue’s theme and aesthetic direction, I chose to work exclusively in black and white. This decision allowed me to emphasize lighting, contrast, and shading while referencing the visual language of classic newspaper comics.

Although developing the four-panel concept came naturally, refining the linework and shading proved to be the most challenging aspect of the project. While striving to emulate the vintage comic style, I realized that maintaining dynamic and natural lighting within a strictly monochromatic palette required more precision than expected, particularly in areas such as the magician’s hat, the theater seating, and the motion of the issue being revealed.

Overall, this project strengthened my understanding of monochromatic composition and contrast-driven storytelling. It was a valuable experience in balancing stylistic inspiration with technical execution, and I look forward to applying more refined lighting and shading techniques in future work.
