About Me

My name is Jasmine Boquer-Velasco (JBV), and I aspire to become an illustrator and graphic designer. I began my work semi-professionally at Leeward Community College’s student publication, Ka Manaʻo, and I hope to continue growing in this field as I work toward earning my Bachelor’s degree in Graphic Design while supporting others in pursuing their creative goals.

If you are interested in using my services, please feel free to reach out for any further details!
